LEMONADE STAND

Game manual

Introduction

You are about to start school in a weeks'. You decided to run a lemonade stand to earn your own pocket money as you do not want to burden your parents. Your goal is to earn as much money while avoiding from making a loss within the week. You planned to run the stand every day in the week from 10am till 6pm.

Weather & Temperature

As the weather temperature becomes hotter, demand for lemonade goes up and customers will pay more for it. However as the weather temperature gets colder, the demand decreases.

Number of Ice Cubes

You should take into consideration the temperature of the weather when determining the number of ice cubes to pour into each cup. Unfortunately in this game, you will have no access to the weather forecast. Hence, you have to assess your risks before deciding whether to check the option to include ice. The journey to the location for your lemonade stand will be long, so there's no turning back once you have made your decision.

Number of Cups of Lemonade

You have to be decisive when choosing how many cups to purchase. Your business for the day will have to close once you run out of lemonade, even if it's before your intended closing time.
